# Customer Segmentation using K-Means

## Objective
Segment customers based on **Annual Income**, **Spending Score**, and **Age** to identify meaningful groups.

---

## Methods Used
- K-Means Clustering  
- Elbow Method (to find optimal clusters)  
- Silhouette Score (to evaluate clustering quality)

## Insight
High income + high spending customers are the most valuable segment and ideal for targeted marketing.
